# Why VinUni

**Source:** https://vinuni.edu.vn/vi/why-vinuni-vi/

VinUni offers a world-class education in Vietnam through strong partnerships with leading global universities, internationally trained faculty, and rigorous interdisciplinary programs. Students benefit from personalized learning, modern facilities, and abundant research, internship, and innovation opportunities.

At VinUni, students are encouraged to become future-ready, socially responsible global citizens.

## Highlights

- Strategic collaborators and global partnerships
- Internationally recognized curriculum
- World-renowned faculty
- Talented student community
- Global learning opportunities at top universities worldwide
- Exclusive internship programs
- Interdisciplinary learning and research
- Creative learning environment exceeding 5-star QS standards

## Academics

VinUni offers **16 degree programs** across Bachelor's, Master's, and PhD levels.

These programs are delivered through **four colleges**:

1. College of Arts & Sciences
2. College of Business & Management
3. College of Engineering & Computer Science
4. College of Health Sciences

Each college emphasizes interdisciplinary learning, hands-on experience, and global standards in education.

## Partnership Network

VinUni partners with well-known institutions such as:

- Cornell SC Johnson College of Business
- University of Pennsylvania
- Nanyang Technological University

The university also collaborates with top universities worldwide for student exchanges and scholarships, and works with industry partners such as Panasonic, Shopee, and ADECCO to support real-world learning and career opportunities.

## Student Life

Student life at VinUniversity is described as vibrant, close-knit, and strongly community-oriented. Campus life is student-driven, with clubs, cultural festivals, and annual events. The university also provides health and well-being services, international student support, and career guidance.

## Scholarship Opportunities

VinUni offers scholarships and financial aid to support access for talented students. Students from 2025–2030 receive a **35% tuition subsidy**, with additional merit-based scholarships that may cover up to **100% of tuition and living costs**. Need-based financial aid may support up to the full cost of attendance.
